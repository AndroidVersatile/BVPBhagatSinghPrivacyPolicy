Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Versatile Prime Infosoft Private Limited ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application, BVP Bhagat Singh Quiz Competition (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes:

  • Account Information: When you log in / signup via WhatsApp Number / Phone Number, we receive your name, dob, class, address, grades, education details, etc.
  • Quiz Data: We collect information about your quiz participation, including answers, scores, time taken, and progress to provide personalized feedback and improve the competition experience.
  • Technical Data: We collect basic device information to ensure compatibility and improve app performance.

2. How We Use Information

  • Authenticating your identity and providing access to your account.
  • Personalizing your user experience and quiz recommendations.
  • Delivering quizzes, calculating scores, and providing feedback.
  • Maintaining and improving the App's stability and competition features.
  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance the quiz competition experience.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for authentication (such as WhatsApp integration) and other functionalities to support the quiz competition features.

4. Data Deletion & Account Closure

We provide a clear pathway for users to delete their accounts and all associated data, including quiz history and personal information, as required by Google Play policy.

How to delete account:

  • Via Contacting Support: You can delete your account by contacting our support team.
  • Via Email: Send a request to support@versatileitsolution.com.

Upon request, we will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 7 business days. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

5. Security

We implement HTTPS encryption to protect your data in transit and secure storage practices for your quiz data and personal information.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our quiz competition app or legal requirements. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
